Magiq360

Magiq360 is an AI video engine that introduces cinematic, spatial camera movements to live cricket
broadcasting — freezing pivotal moments in time and seamlessly flying the viewer's perspective
from one camera angle to another, delivering unparalleled visual storytelling

Cinematic Action Replays

Transforms standard replays into captivating, high-end cinematic sequences. By centering on key moments and panning across the best perspectives while the action is paused, it elevates the drama of the game

Universal Camera Compatibility

Camera Fly is completely camera-agnostic. It works with the broadcaster's existing camera layout, working on any existing camera feed without additional hardware or infrastructure changes

Live-Production Speed

Designed specifically for the high-pressure environment of live cricket. The AI generation is optimised for fast turnaround, ensuring directors can deploy Camera Fly sequences without disrupting the broadcast flow
